About Tanaka Guitar
Tanaka Guitar is a famous guitar store established in August 2021. The
first shop was established at Yogyakarta and was founded by a famous
japanese voice actor, Aimi Tanaka. Nowadays, there are a lot of branches all
over Indonesia. Our store provides high-quality guitars from many brands.
